Get me to the Beer

2022-01-14

At some point during the lockdown it was announced that we could meet in groups of no more than 8 in an outdoor setting. This meant that we would need to have knowledge of where we could meet our friends for the first time in an age. I’m sure we know of our local and well frequented boozers; but what about those that have outdoor seating? How would we know where to meet our friends?

Luckily thanks to the likes of reddit and other crowd-sourced websites, I was able to come across a PDF of boozers:

A PDF like this is nice, but this isn’t too useful in it’s present form. Wouldn’t it be nice if we were able to see where these pubs are on a map? Luckily this is achievable using:

  • pdftools
  • regex to pull out postcodes
  • PostcodesioR to get lat/lon co-ordinates from the postcode
  • leaflet to visualise it all

Here’s how the first few rows look after using pdftools to parse it:

Boozer Name & Address Region Part of London Outside Space postcode lat lng

The Scolt Head, 107A Culford Rd, London N1 4HT E Haggerston Space at Front N1 4HT 51.54379 -0.081569
